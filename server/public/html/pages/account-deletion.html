Account Deletion Request

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to delete your Semaphore account and associated data, please send an email to the contact address below with the following information:

Email Request Details

Write an email to aravindmathradan2@gmail.com

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Semaphore"
  • Your username or the email address associated with your account
  • A brief reason for account deletion (optional)

We will process your request as soon as possible, typically within 30 days, and send you a confirmation email once your account and associated data have been deleted.

What Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account profile information
  • Your email address and authentication details
  • Your feed subscriptions and preferences
  • Your reading history and saved items
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention

Some information might be retained in our backup systems for a limited period of time, but will not be available for use. Additionally, we may retain certain information if necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.
